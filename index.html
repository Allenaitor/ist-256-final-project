  • “Born to Burn!”

    Eruptor is a force of nature, hailing from a species that lived deep in the underground of a floating volcanic island until a massive eruption launched their entire civilization to the surface. He's a complete hot head -- steaming, fuming, and quite literally erupting over almost anything. To help control his temper, he likes to relax in lava pools, particularly because there are no crowds.

    Check them out!

  • “Fear the Fish!”

    Gill Grunt, a courageous member of the Gillmen military, embarked on a quest for adventure and encountered a captivating mermaid in a misty lagoon among the clouds. He pledged to return to her after his service but discovered upon his return years later that she had been abducted by pirates. Despite his heartbreak, Gill Grunt vowed to find her and joined the Skylanders to protect others from similar evils, all while remaining vigilant in his search for the mermaid and her captors.

    Check them out!

  • “Twists of Fury!”

    Whirlwind, an air dragon with unicorn heritage, struggled to fit in due to her unique blend of traits. Despite being ostracized by both dragons and unicorns, she found solace in the stormy clouds, where she honed her tempest powers. When trolls threatened both species, Whirlwind defended them fiercely, unleashing a stunning rainbow of power that struck fear into evildoers across Skylands. Since then, her presence signaled impending doom for villains, as they fled from the rainbow that followed her storms.

    Check them out!

  • “Blinded by the Light!”

    Flashwing knows just how beautiful she appears and also how deadly she can be. She often tries to steal the spotlight from her fellow Skylanders, but her kind heart always shines through in the end.


    Flashwing's true origins are a mystery.

    Check them out!

  • “Slice and Dice!”

    With his skeletal frame, cold voice, and tremendous abilities, Chop Chop is often assumed to be a bit of a frightening fellow. In truth, he is a very frightening fellow. Despite that however, he appears to possess a sense of honor, as shown by his dedication to finding those who created him.

    Check them out!
