  • "Selling Figures at Reasonable Prices!"

    Skylanders Portal Gateway (SPG) is a home business that started as an avenue to sell duplicate figures obtained in lots I purchased. After seeing how much these figures soled separately, I decided to offer respectable deals that I would appreciate seeing from other vendors. Make sure to see our current listings and find your Skylander today!

    Check Listings!

  • “Working Figures or Your Money Back!”

    We inspect and test every figure to ensure you are receiving the figure you were promised. However, keep in mind that most figures listed here are pre-owned so they may be subjected to some minor wear. Please view the picture and description of the figure listed before making a purchase inquiry.

    Check Listings!

  • “Local Delivery”

    Currently we mainly offer local delivery though meet-ups in a determined time and location. This is done to keep prices as low as possible for local customers. We occasionally do ship at certain locations however that is paid by the customer. For any doubts, please fill out a form in our "contact" page for more information!

    Contact Us!

  • “Dear customers!”

    Thank you for your business with Skylanders Portal Gateway! If you wish to buy a figure from our stock, please go to our "contact" page and fill out the form with your desired figures. We will connect you with one of our sellers to coordinate a convenient meet-up time and location.

    Fill out Form!

  • “Looking to Sell?”

    If you are a fellow collector looking to sell a few figures from your personal collection, we are more willing to make a deal with you to help them find a new home. Please go to our "contact" page and fill out the form with your contact information. We will connect you with one of our representative to coordinate a fair and equal deal for both parties.
